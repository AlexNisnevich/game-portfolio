Untrusted
Short description
“Untrusted” (2014) is a lo-fi cyberpunk adventure game that challenges the player to escape from a series of 21 increasingly cryptic puzzle rooms by editing the very code that is running the game in the web browser.

Untrusted was initially geared toward programmers and puzzle fans, but has also been used in an educational context to teach programming in the U.S., Scotland, India, and Colombia. It has been played over 800,000 times in 177 countries, and has been ranked among the Top 100 Online Games by PC World magazine.

I was the co-designer and co-developer of Untrusted with Greg Shuflin.

Shattered Worlds
Short description
“Not a Soul in the World” (2012) is a text-based adventure game in which you play the part of a psychologist talking to the first mind successfully uploaded into a computer, who has come to believe that he is an AI program and not a human. Your objective is to make the patient realize that he is human, by uncovering powerful memories from his past.

Not A Soul in the World is a web game aimed at fans of experimental adventure games, particularly those curious about implications of artificial intelligence. It was created for Ludum Dare #22, where it was rated #84 in the Mood category out of 717 entries.

I was the sole designer and developer of Not a Soul in the World.

Short description
“A Snack in Every Port” (2014) was an interactive potluck entry. In it, a paper map and a compass were presented to each player, who in turn traced out the path of a ship on the map. Where the ship landed determined which of several dishes each player could eat.

A Snack in Every Port was created for Appetite Obscure #8, a conceptual dinner, with a target audience of experimental art lovers.

I was the sole designer and developer of A Snack in Every Port.

Short description
“Shattered Worlds” (2014) is a puzzle-platformer game in which you play as a lost sprite looking for his home game, all while collecting pieces of other games. You progress through the game by overlaying levels you have completed on top of the current level, turning previously encountered obstacles into tools for the player.

Shattered Worlds is a web game with a broad target audience. It was created for Ludum Dare #31, where it was rated #46 in Theme and #48 in Innovation out of 1045 entries.

I was the lead developer in a team of seven for Shattered Worlds.
